  • Research Interests

    I am interested in applying economic modeling, optimization theory and algorithms, and statistical data analysis to study problems related to decision making. By using these tools, I study the impact of incentives and information in environments with complicated operations. With the research findings, decision support systems can then be built for decision optimization. Currently my works mainly lie in the following fields:

  • Economics of Information.
  • Revenue and Operations Management.
  • Electronic Commerce.
  • Healthcare Management.

    In our deparment, my students and I together run the Lab of Information Economics and Decision Optimization to study problems in the above fields. While we pursue academic contributions in theory, we always try to keep our research aligned with industry needs. Current industry partners of our lab include:

  • One 3C retailer in Taiwan (for inventory allocation and reward mechanisms).
  • One theater in Taiwan (for ticking pricing).
  • One metropolitan teaching hospital in Taiwan (for equipment maintenance and contracting).

    All prospective graduate students who are interested in our lab are more than welcome to contact me or other lab members directly for more information. Undergraduate students are also welcome. I am also open to all kinds of research ideas.

  • 我的研究主要是利用經濟模型、最佳化理論與演算法以及統計資料分析來探討各種決策問題。利用這些工具，我研究在複雜的作業環境中，誘因與資訊會如何影響最佳決策的產生。有了這些研究成果，我們可以建立決策支援系統以求決策最佳化。下面是一些我正在探討的研究領域：

  • 資訊經濟。
  • 收益與作業管理。
  • 電子商務。
  • 醫療照護管理。

    在臺大資管系，我的學生們和我一起經營資訊經濟與決策最佳化實驗室，並且研究上述這些議題。在追求理論面的學術貢獻的同時，我們也努力讓研究能與產業實務接軌。此刻我們的業界夥伴包括：

  • 臺灣一家 3C 產品零售商（存貨分配與獎勵機制）。
  • 臺灣一家劇團（票券訂價）。
  • 臺灣一家區域教學醫院（設備維護與合約制定）。

    如果你即將進入臺大資管所就讀，並且對上述議題或方法感興趣，非常歡迎你直接與我或其它實驗室成員聯絡，以獲得更多資訊！大學部學生如果有興趣做這方面的研究或學習，也非常歡迎。如果你有自己的研究想法，我也很樂意與你討論。
